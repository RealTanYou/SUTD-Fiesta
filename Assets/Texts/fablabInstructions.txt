How to play:
You are in the fab lab! Collect things that are permitted in the fab lab to gain points. If you collect forbidden items, you lose points.

Points List:
Permitted Items:
SUTD Card (+120)
Long Pants (+60)
Jeans (+45)
Sneakers (+25)

Forbidden Items:
Shorts (-35)
Heels (-30)
Drink (-20)
Skirt (-20)
Slippers (-10)
